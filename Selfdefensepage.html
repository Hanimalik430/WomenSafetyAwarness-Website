Self Defense Basics

"Learn simple ways to protect yourself and stay confident."

Basic Self Defense
  • Learn simple blocking techniques.
  • Use your voice to attract attention.
  • Target weak points like eyes and knees.
  • Practice self-defense regularly.
Awareness Techniqiue
  • Stay alert in public places
  • Keep distance from strangers
  • Trust your instincts
  • Avoid risky situations
  • Stay in well-lit areas
Confidence Building
  • Walk with confidence and good posture.
  • Speak clearly and firmly.
  • Practice decision-making skills.
  • Believe in your ability to stay safe.
Safe Escape Tips
  • Look for the nearest safe exit.
  • Run toward crowded areas for help.
  • Keep emergency contacts ready.
  • Avoid carrying heavy items while escaping.
Emergency Actions
  • Call emergency services immediately.
  • Stay calm during emergencies.
  • Share your location with trusted people.
  • Move to a safe and secure place.
